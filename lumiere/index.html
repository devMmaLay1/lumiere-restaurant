Meet The Chef

Étienne Marchand

"Every plate that leaves my kitchen carries a piece of my story."

Born in Lyon and trained across the great kitchens of Paris, Chef Marchand brings three decades of passion to every dish at Lumière. His philosophy is simple — honour the ingredient, respect the tradition, and never stop evolving.

At Lumière, he has assembled a brigade of exceptional talent united by a shared belief that fine dining should be both extraordinary and deeply personal.

Michelin Star 2019 · Le Cordon Bleu Paris · 30 Years Experience
